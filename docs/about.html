About Us

At Data Thread Inc., we sit at the intersection of financial markets, data engineering, and heavy industry.

Our Edge: Full-Stack Thinking

From business problem → data model & architecture → trading / operational logic → dashboards and workflows.

We combine engineering discipline (process design, risk, safety) with quant/trading practicality (latency, fees, slippage, margin).

We're not a slideware consultancy. We build models and systems that plug into your existing stack and show up in your P&L, KPIs, and uptime.

Our Experience Spans:

  • Algorithmic trading & market making across centralized and decentralized exchanges, managing strategy design, execution pipelines, and P&L optimization for multi-hundred-million AUM environments.
  • Digital transformation and analytics inside large industrial operations (potash, nitrogen, phosphate), delivering forecasting tools, dashboards, and computer-vision projects.
  • Trading systems development for FX, derivatives, and crypto, including REST-based execution platforms, data servers, and real-time analytics.
