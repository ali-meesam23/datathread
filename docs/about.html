About

Full-Stack Thinking. Shared Success.

At Data Thread Inc., we sit at the intersection of financial markets, data engineering, and heavy industry.
Our edge is full-stack thinking:

  • From business problem → data model & architecture → trading / operational logic → dashboards and workflows.
  • Combining engineering discipline (process design, risk, safety) with quant/trading practicality (latency, fees, slippage, margin).

Our Experience Spans

Algorithmic Trading & Market Making

Across centralized and decentralized exchanges, managing strategy design, execution pipelines, and P&L optimization for multi-hundred-million AUM environments.

Digital Transformation & Analytics

Inside large industrial operations (potash, nitrogen, phosphate), delivering forecasting tools, dashboards, and computer-vision projects.

Trading Systems Development

For FX, derivatives, and crypto, including REST-based execution platforms, data servers, and real-time analytics.

We're not a slideware consultancy. We build models and systems that plug into your existing stack and show up in your P&L, KPIs, and uptime.
