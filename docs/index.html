How It Works

A simple 4-step consultation workflow.

1

Discovery (free intro call)

30–45 minutes to understand your context, constraints, and goals. Quick assessment: what's realistic in 3–6 months vs. long-term.

2

Diagnostic & proposal

Short diagnostic (1–2 working sessions + data / system review). We define: problem statement and success metrics, scope options (MVP vs. full build), timeline, responsibilities, and fees. You receive a written proposal you can share with your team.

3

Build & implementation

We work in clearly defined phases (design → build → validate → deploy). Weekly or bi-weekly check-ins with simple dashboards or progress reports. We coordinate with your internal teams (tech, ops, finance, compliance).

4

Handover & ongoing support

Documentation and training for your team. Optional retainer for monitoring, enhancements, and future projects. Periodic reviews to ensure the system or model still matches your reality.
