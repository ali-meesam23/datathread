Digital Transformation & Data Foundations

Seamless, data-driven transformation that enhances operational efficiency and delivers actionable insights.

  • Mapping current workflows (trading, ops, finance, or plant operations).
  • Designing target data architecture (sources, pipelines, storage, access patterns).
  • Implementing or specifying:
    • ETL/ELT pipelines and data models.

  • Governance: access, logging, and documentation so it's auditable.

Trading firms and desks drowning in spreadsheets and manual reconciliations. Industrial or mining operations still reliant on paper, email chains, and siloed systems. Founders and COOs who know they're not getting enough value from the data they already have.

Financial Modelling & Decision Support

Robust financial models that provide decision-grade insights for strategic planning and risk management.

  • Clarifying the business question (pricing, expansion, hedging, product strategy).
  • Building robust models for:
    • Pricing and margin analysis.
    • Commodity / FX / crypto exposure and hedging.
    • Revenue and cashflow forecasts with scenario analysis.
  • Validating and stress-testing model assumptions.
  • Packaging models into user-friendly tools (Excel, dashboards, or apps).
  • Handover sessions and documentation.

CFOs, Heads of Finance, and founders who need decision-grade numbers, not best guesses. Commodity producers and traders needing robust price / volume scenarios. Funds and trading shops evaluating new products or strategies.

Trading System Architecture & Review

Architectural excellence for trading systems that deliver reliability, scalability, and performance.

  • Reviewing current trading stack:
    • Connectivity (APIs, FIX, websockets).
    • Strategy code and data flows.
    • Risk and monitoring.
  • Designing or refining:
    • System architecture (data server, execution engine, monitoring layer).
    • Strategy deployment workflows and change control.
    • Risk limits, guardrails, and alerting.
  • Prototyping or specifying:
    • New bots or strategies (e.g., MM, arb, execution algos).
    • Latency and robustness improvements.

Proprietary trading firms and family offices. Market makers and liquidity providers in crypto and FX. Founders with working bots that "sort of work" but lack structure, monitoring, or scalability.
