How It Works

A simple 4-step consultation workflow designed for clarity and results

1

Discovery (Free Intro Call)

30–45 minutes to understand your context, constraints, and goals. We'll conduct a quick assessment of what's realistic in 3–6 months vs. long-term. This call is free and no-obligation — it's about understanding whether we're a good fit for each other.

2

Diagnostic & Proposal

Short diagnostic phase (1–2 working sessions + data / system review). We define: problem statement and success metrics, scope options (MVP vs. full build), timeline, responsibilities, and fees. You receive a written proposal you can share with your team for alignment and approval.

3

Build & Implementation

We work in clearly defined phases (design → build → validate → deploy). Weekly or bi-weekly check-ins with simple dashboards or progress reports keep everyone aligned. We coordinate with your internal teams (tech, ops, finance, compliance) to ensure smooth integration.

4

Handover & Ongoing Support

Complete documentation and training for your team so the knowledge stays in-house. Optional retainer available for monitoring, enhancements, and future projects. We conduct periodic reviews to ensure the system or model still matches your evolving reality.

Client Benefits

Clients typically see benefits in four key areas

1. Clearer Decisions

Reliable models and dashboards instead of ad-hoc spreadsheets. Transparent assumptions so decision-makers can challenge and refine strategies with confidence.

2. Stronger Systems

Trading and operational systems that are architected, not patched together. Reduced operational risk from manual steps and hidden single points of failure.

3. Better Performance

More stable P&L, tighter forecasts, and improved capital efficiency in trading and operations. Throughput increases, downtime reductions, or improved safety where industrial projects are in scope.

4. Knowledge Transfer

Your team understands the why and how behind models and systems. Documentation and training mean you're not locked into a black box — you own the capability.

You're not just buying a report; you're buying capability that remains in your organization.
